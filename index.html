How It Works

End-to-end pipeline from image upload to AI prediction

STEP 01

Image Upload

Biopsy image is converted to base64 and sent to our Vercel serverless function.

STEP 02

Preprocessing

Resized to 224×224 (classification) or 128×128 (segmentation). Normalized with ImageNet statistics.

STEP 03

Inference + TTA

4-rotation TTA for classification (8 passes). D4 TTA for segmentation (16 passes). Results averaged.

STEP 04

Output

Classification: predicted class (0–11) + confidence. Segmentation: binary mask overlay on original image.
