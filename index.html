How to Use

1
Get your image

Take a close-up dermatoscopy photo of the skin lesion. The image should show only the lesion area clearly.

2
Pick a task below

Classification — the AI tells you what type of lesion it is.
Segmentation — the AI highlights exactly where the lesion is.

3
Upload the image

Drag your image onto the upload area, or click it to open a file browser.

4
Click the button & wait

Press the green button. Results appear in about 10–30 seconds. Do not close the page.

How It Works

End-to-end pipeline from image upload to AI prediction

STEP 01

Image Upload

Dermatoscopy image is converted to base64 and sent to our Vercel serverless function.

STEP 02

Preprocessing

Resized to 224×224 (classification) or 128×128 (segmentation). Normalized with ImageNet statistics.

STEP 03

Inference + TTA

4-rotation TTA for classification (8 passes). D4 TTA for segmentation (16 passes). Results averaged.

STEP 04

Output

Classification: predicted class (0–11) + confidence. Segmentation: binary mask overlay on original image.
